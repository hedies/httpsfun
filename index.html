What is https?

Http is a communication protocol that is used to transfer webbsites on the world wide web. It works as a question/answer function.

Https includes the security program that creates more secure connections and transfers. Https is used for payment transaction on the Internet, e-mail and transactions of sensitive information.

Why should I use https?

  • Encryption: data can't be bugged.

  • Data privacy: data can't be changed or corrupted without it being detected.

  • Authentication: proves that the users are communicating with the right website.
